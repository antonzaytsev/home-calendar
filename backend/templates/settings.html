How to get your iCloud Webcal URL:

On Mac/iPhone/iPad:

  1. Open the Calendar app
  2. Right-click (or long-press) on the calendar you want to share
  3. Select "Share Calendar"
  4. Choose "Public Calendar"
  5. Copy the webcal:// URL that appears

On iCloud.com:

  1. Go to iCloud.com and open Calendar
  2. Click the share icon next to your calendar
  3. Enable "Public Calendar"
  4. Copy the webcal:// URL
